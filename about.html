Who we are

SWIPELAB is an independent software studio. We build the foundational parts of software most teams buy off the shelf — compilers, runtimes and developer tools — and the products that run on top of them.

It started with a conviction: the best products come from owning the stack. So we built sx, a native systems language with an LLVM backend — then proved it by shipping real software in it: a match-3 game (m3te), a cross-platform release platform (distribution), and a chess engine (game).

sx is open source under the MIT license, and the products built on it keep the language honest. No committees, no roadmaps written for show — just small teams, tight feedback loops, and tools we’d actually want to use: fast, predictable, and close to the machine.

What we believe

Three convictions show up in everything we ship.

OWN THE STACK

We don't wait for the right tool, we build it. sx exists because off-the-shelf wasn't good enough.

SHIP TO PROVE IT

A language is talk until something real runs on it. Every product we build keeps the compiler honest.

SMALL AND FAST

No committees, no theatre. Small teams, tight loops, and decisions made by the people writing the code.
